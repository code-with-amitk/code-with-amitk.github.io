DLP (Data Loss Prevention)

if sensitive data is leaked(on cloud), then DLP protects the data by inspecting the cloud services.
Data detected by Netskope DLP (Data Loss Prevention) includes sensitive information such as personally identifiable information (adhar, PAN etc), payment card information (PCI), passwords, credentials, and confidential business data.

How it works?

Uploading Credit card
